3. LEAVE POLICY
----------------------------------------------------

Annual Leave Entitlement:
  - Paid Leaves (PL): 18 days per year (1.5 per month, accrued)
  - Sick Leaves (SL): 12 days per year (no carryover)
  - Casual Leaves (CL): 6 days per year (no carryover)
  - Public Holidays: As per the annual holiday calendar (published in January each year)

Carryover:
  - Up to 10 unused Paid Leaves can be carried over to the next year
  - Sick and Casual Leaves expire on December 31 and cannot be carried over
  - Maximum leave balance cap: 30 Paid Leaves

How to Apply:
  1. Log in to the HRMS portal at https://hrms.nexora.com
  2. Navigate to "Leave Management" → "Apply Leave"
  3. Select leave type, dates, and provide a reason
  4. Submit — your manager will approve/reject within 48 hours
  5. For emergency leaves, inform your manager via Teams/call and apply retroactively within 2 days

Special Leaves:
  - Maternity Leave: 26 weeks (as per Maternity Benefit Act)
  - Paternity Leave: 10 working days
  - Bereavement Leave: 5 working days (immediate family)
  - Marriage Leave: 3 working days (once during employment)

8. IT SETUP GUIDE
----------------------------------------------------

Laptop Setup (Day 1):
  1. Power on and sign in with your Nexora email credentials (provided by IT)
  2. Connect to "Nexora-Corp" WiFi (password provided at the IT desk)
  3. Install all pending Windows/macOS updates
  4. Open the Company Portal app — it will auto-install required software

VPN Setup:
  1. Download GlobalProtect from the Company Portal
  2. Open GlobalProtect and enter the portal address: vpn.nexora.com
  3. Sign in with your Nexora email and password
  4. Click Connect — you should see "Connected" status within 30 seconds
  5. If it fails, check that your MFA (Microsoft Authenticator) is set up correctly

Email & Calendar:
  1. Open Outlook (pre-installed)
  2. Sign in with your Nexora email
  3. Your team calendar and meeting invites will sync automatically
  4. Set your working hours in Outlook: Settings → Calendar → Working Hours

GitHub Access:
  1. Email your GitHub username to your tech lead
  2. You'll receive an invite to the Nexora Enterprise organization within 24 hours
  3. Clone repositories using: git clone https://github.com/nexora-tech/{repo-name}
  4. Always create feature branches — never push directly to main

9. FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
----------------------------------------------------

Q: How do I reset my password?
A: Go to https://passwordreset.nexora.com or contact IT at ithelp@nexora.com.

Q: Who do I contact for payroll issues?
A: Email payroll@nexora.com or raise a ticket on the HRMS portal.

Q: Can I use my personal laptop for work?
A: No. All work must be done on company-issued devices for security compliance.

Q: How do I book a meeting room?
A: Use Outlook Calendar → New Event → Add Room → select from available rooms.

Q: What's the notice period?
A: 60 days for all employees. Can be negotiated with HR in special circumstances.

Q: How do I claim reimbursements?
A: Upload receipts on HRMS portal → "Claims" → select category → submit. Approved claims are processed in the next salary cycle.

Q: Is there a referral bonus?
A: Yes. ₹25,000 for junior roles, ₹50,000 for senior roles, ₹75,000 for leadership roles. Paid after the referred employee completes 90 days.

Q: Where do I find the holiday calendar?
A: HRMS portal → "Company Policies" → "Holiday Calendar 2026"
